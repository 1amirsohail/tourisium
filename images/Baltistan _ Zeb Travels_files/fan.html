To all our trusted customers and friends.
As many of you may already know, the novel coronavirus known as Covid-19 has managed to spread to every corner of the world and has successfully halted the tourism industries of most countries. We at Zeb Travels understand the direness of the situation and our hearts go out to all those affected by this pandemic. While our offices currently remain shut, our team is doing its part on a personal level (from social distancing to wearing... masks) in order to slow down the spread of this disease. Although Pakistan's travel and tourism industry has been severely affected by Covid-19, we hope that it sees a quick recovery once this pandemic has passed. Rest assured, Zeb Travels will be there to offer our services and make your next trip special and unique once the situation improves. Until then we hope that you stay safe and healthy and hope to be able to make your next trip great, once this passes.

And to all our customers, we wish you a happy and peaceful Ramadan!
